Why do we need it?

We need Universal Design to make the digital content accessible for everyone. So much of our world lives online now, and designing digital content with accessiblity in mind ensures online information can reach the widest range of people possible.

Accessiblity is incorporated into many different domains, from building construction to concerts. People who create digital content needs to follow suit to make sure nobody is left behind in the digital age.

Fun fact: 82% of customers with disabilities said they spend more on accessible websites.
